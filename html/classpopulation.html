template<typename _ty, typename _realTy>
class population< _ty, _realTy >

Class that encapsulates the concept of a population in a Genetic algorithm optimization method.

Importantan Variables:

_myPop: Container that stores the individuals of the population _bestId,_worseId: Respectivily best and worse individuals of the consteiner _myPop _avarege: Avarage value of the objective function of the population
